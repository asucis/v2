About

Shaping international culture at ASU

Coalition of International Students or CIS is one of the 7 identity/cultural based coalitions at Arizona State University that forms the Council of Coalitions. Within CIS is 19 partner organizations registered within Arizona State University as members of cultural, identity-based and/or diversity based student organizations.
